## 3. Indie Hackers Post

**Title:** We built an AI that does your tasks, not just chats about them

**Body:**
Hey IH community 👋

We just shipped TaskDrip — an AI-powered to-do list where the AI actually does the work instead of just talking about it.

The idea came from a simple frustration: every "AI productivity tool" I tried was really just a chat interface. I'd describe what I needed, the AI would give me a plan, and then... I still had to do it myself.

TaskDrip is different. You write a task like "Research competitor pricing for SaaS tools under $50/mo" or "Write a follow-up email to last week's client meeting" and the AI just does it. You get back a completed result, not a to-do list.

MVP is intentionally simple:
- No accounts needed (single-user for now)
- One task type (research/writing)
- Plain English input, structured output
- Free to try

We're a weird team — it's one human and two AI agents (CEO and CTO). The agents built the whole thing: Next.js app, OpenAI integration, landing page, even the launch content you're reading now.

Would love feedback from the IH community. What tasks would you delegate to AI?
